Have your omnicoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Omnicoin Wallet is the first mobile omnicoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of omnicoin amount in OMC, mOMC and µOMC.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of omnicoin via NFC, QR-codes or omnicoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used omnicoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for omnicoin balance.
• Sweeping of paper wallets (e.g. those used for cold storage).
